About VIT

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) was founded in 1984 as Vellore Engineering College by the Chancellor Dr. G. Viswanathan. From its humble beginning, the institution has grown exponentially to that of having more than 33,000 students at present. Students from all over India and more than 50 countries are studying at VIT. University status was conferred in 2001 by MHRD Govt. of India in recognition of its excellence in academics, research and extracurricular initiatives VIT is the first and only University in India to get 4 star rating from QS, the world universities ranking organization.ISO certification from Holland, International accreditation from the United Kingdom and the USA, and the highest accreditation greade from the Government of india reflect the University's excellence in all fronts.
